## What it does
**Search Location Changer** lets you see exactly what users in other countries and regions see when they search Google. 

- **Reliable Localized SERPs:** Changes the Google search location parameters without affecting your real IP or physical location.
- **Seamless Experience:** Works natively via a lightweight Chrome extension popup.
- **Non-Disruptive:** Opens a new tab with results tailored to your selected region, leaving your current workflow and default tab completely untouched.

*Note: This is not a VPN—it is a purpose-built search location changer designed for accuracy and speed.*

## How it works
1. **Install** the Chrome extension to your browser.
2. **Click** the extension icon in your toolbar.
3. **Select** your desired country (and language/region if supported).
4. **Search** directly or click to open a pre-built search URL.

*Your default SERP tab remains unchanged; the extension automatically opens your localized results in a fresh new tab.*

## FAQ

**Does this change my real IP or location?**
No. It only modifies the search location parameters sent to Google for your specific query. Your device's real IP and network location remain completely unchanged.

**Does it work for other search engines?**
Currently, the extension is optimized exclusively for Google search results to provide the most accurate region representations.

**Is it free?**
Yes, it is entirely free to use and fully open-source.

**Is it safe to use?**
Absolutely. The codebase is fully transparent and auditable on GitHub. We don't track your search history or collect personal data.

**How does it handle languages in multi-language regions?**
You can easily select both the country and the language/region settings within the extension to ensure you get precisely the localized language results you need.
