## Perfect Crops Every Time
Don't let bad framing ruin a great photo. Our Image Cropper helps you focus on the most important part of your image.

### Preset Ratios
We include popular aspect ratios to make your life easier:
- **Square (1:1)**: Perfect for Instagram posts and profile pictures.
- **16:9**: The standard for YouTube thumbnails and video covers.
- **4:3**: Classic photography ratio.
- **Free**: Crop exactly how you want with no restrictions.

### Why Use a Cropper?
- **Composition**: Improve the visual balance of your photos utilizing the rule of thirds.
- **Focus**: Remove distracting background elements.
- **Standardization**: Ensure all images on your website or profile are uniform in size.
