## Why convert SVG to PNG?
While SVGs are perfect for the web due to infinite scalability, sometimes you need a raster format like PNG for:
- **Social Media**: Uploading to platforms that don't support SVG.
- **Favicons**: Legacy browser support.
- **Email Signatures**: Ensuring consistent rendering across email clients.

This tool runs entirely in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to a server being 100% private.
